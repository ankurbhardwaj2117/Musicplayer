    A universally recognised Bollywood artist, Arijit Singh’s dexterity extends to being a singer, composer, music producer and an extremely talented live performer. Cited as one of the most accomplished singers from India, Arijit’s iconic repertoire consists of over 1000 songs. His chart-topper songs like “Tum Hi Ho”, “Channa Mereya”, “Phir Le Aya Dil”, “Duaa”, and “Kabira”, are just a few that every Bollywood fan has memorised. His soulful, soothing and melodious voice yet rough vocal textures and charismatic personality allow him to mesmerise his audience, worldwide. Being one of the most in-demand performers across the country, Arijit has constantly maintained his peak position in the Bollywood Music Industry. As evident from his live performances, he has managed to sing Bollywood classics effortlessly and shine on every stage. As a voice of the new generation, Arijit has lent his voice to all popular actors and has worked with most of the top composers in the Bollywood music industry. Starting his own record label in 2020, Oriyon Music, he continues to awe his listeners through ever-evolving music style and vocal skills and will keep the music going!
